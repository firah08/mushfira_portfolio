I am a Year 2 Business & Financial Technology student at Nanyang Polytechnic. I'm someone who enjoys taking on new challenges, whether that's building software, analysing financial information or engaging with the community through volunteering. I enjoy learning continuously and finding practical ways technology can improve business processes and everyday experiences.

Through my coursework and personal projects, I have developed skills in financial analysis, programming and web development. I enjoy learning how digital technologies can improve decision-making, streamline financial processes and create better user experiences.

Beyond academics, I actively volunteer with Sengkang North Youth Network, where I have organised community events, emceed programmes and engaged residents through outreach initiatives. These experiences have strengthened my communication, teamwork, leadership and confidence when working with people from diverse backgrounds.

I believe growth comes from continuously challenging myself through new experiences. Whether I'm building software, analysing financial data or supporting community events, I enjoy learning, contributing to meaningful projects and developing skills that prepare me for a career in finance and FinTech.
